Swipe Right For Your Health: Reducing Hospital Related Infections With Wearable Tech

Medicine isn't perfect, and everyone understands that it's possible for a condition to worsen somewhat over the course of treatment. But imagine instead that you went to the hospital and came

down with an unrelated illness that was much more dangerous than the one you already had! Sadly, healthcare associated infections (HAI's) are commonplace in hospitals and other treatment facilities


around the world. In fact, the Center For Disease Control (CDC) reports that nearly one in twenty five patients in the US are affected by HAI's, which collectively resulted in over 205 deaths a day in 2011.
